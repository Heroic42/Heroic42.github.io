Title Page Jujitsu Philosophy Jujitsu Techniques

These are some of the basic technique families in jujitsu's art.

Kansetsu waza: Joint locks

Ukemi waza: Break falls

Katame waza: Grappling

Nage waza: Throws

Atemi waza: Strikes

Shime waza: Chokes

Kyusho: Pressure points

Each family contains multiple techniques and variations to achieve its stated goal.

There are whole styles of jujitsu dedicated to one or multiple of these technique families

i.e. Aikido focuses on Kansetsu waza and Nage waza, and Brazilian JuJitsu focuses on

Ukemi waza, Nage waza, and Katame waza. Mastering these techniques takes quite a long

time, and mastery can take commitment to a lifelong study of the arts.
